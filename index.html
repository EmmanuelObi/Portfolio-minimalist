about me

Emmanuel Obi has been programming for around 2 years and tutoring for over 6 months. He is a Frontend Developer, Technical Writer, Tutor and an Electrical Engineering Major who loves to code. He specializes in everything from HTML5 to front end frameworks like React. Emmanuel has mastered the art of learning and always loves to share his knowledge.

Emmanuel has created a blog called CodeProphet where he publishes articles about himself and african youths in general on Software Engineering and Career Growth. Emmanuel is passionate about the tech community and supports inclusion for women in technology. He co-organizes community events and speaks at technology meetups on his campus.
